Multi-Agent ChatBot with AWS Strands Framework

  • Design orchestrator-driven multi-agent workflows where intent detection routes conversations to domain-specific specialist agents.
  • Implement identity verification & validation gates, state management, explicit user confirmation before backend/API execution, and structured response contracts for reliable enterprise chatbot behavior.
  • Engineer prompts and agent instructions as modular, versionable assets with deterministic flow control, guardrail-aware behavior, and clear escalation paths.
  • Build AI observability into runtime using OpenTelemetry traces, structured logs, CloudWatch dashboards, custom business metrics, and PII masking patterns.
  • Create evaluation suites for agent quality using Strands Evals, including helpfulness, relevance, execution time, tool-call trajectory, state transitions, and final response validation.

Smart Complaint Classifier & Prioritizer for Guest Complaints

  • Designed, developed a Generative AI solution using LLaMA models to automate guest complaint classification and sentiment analysis, delivering real-time customer insights with 85-90% classification accuracy.
  • Automated guest complaint triage and prioritization workflows using GenAI, reducing manual review effort by 60%+ and significantly improving customer response times and service experience.
  • Enabled near real-time identification of customer pain points and emerging service trends, empowering business teams to take proactive corrective actions and improve customer satisfaction.
  • Reduced complaint processing turnaround time through AI-driven categorization and sentiment scoring, improving operational efficiency and accelerating issue resolution.
  • Delivered scalable AI-powered analytics capabilities capable of processing large volumes of guest feedback, supporting enterprise-wide customer experience initiatives.
